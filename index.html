Enterprise Software

We provide innovative software solutions tailored for AEC companies, featuring our flagship software, APPIA. APPIA extends the capability of Civil 3D with thousands of advanced functions, designed to significantly improve both the efficiency and quality of your work. Additionally, we offer expert consulting services to develop custom software solutions that meet the unique needs of your company.

Engineering Services for Home and Business Owners

We specialize in fire-safety and sustainability consulting, offering a comprehensive range of services to help safeguard your property and reduce long-term costs. Our expertise includes designing greywater systems, performing energy analyses for cost savings, and providing flood and fire safety consulting to protect your property from natural disasters and lower your insurance rates. Additionally, we provide a wide array of traditional civil engineering services, including pond and drainage design, rain-catchment systems, green-roofs, utility design, well and retaining structure design, and custom home planning, as well as thorough due diligence reports.

Cutting-Edge Research

At our core, we are a community of researchers, engineers, and scientists aiming to push the boundaries of the AEC industry. We hope to leverage the functionality of artificial intelligence and neural networks to automate the design of traditional civil engineering systems, so that engineers can focus on revolutionizing the built-environment rather than monotonous and expensive tasks.
